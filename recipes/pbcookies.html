Peanut Butter Cookies

Description

Delicious soft peanut butter flavored cookies.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar, 1/4 cup additional to roll cookies
  • 1 stick butter, at room temperature
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup smooth peanut butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 1/2 cups flour
